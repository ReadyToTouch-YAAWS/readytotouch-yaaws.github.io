How to use it
01
Explore Job Openings
In companies based in the USA, UK, and EU.
02
Find Company Profiles
Visit the LinkedIn profiles of these companies and copy their profile links.
03
Add to Your List
Paste the copied links into the tool to build your list of target companies.
04
Discover Professionals
The tool will generate links to the LinkedIn profiles of Ukrainian professionals already working in these companies.
05
Connect and Network
Reach out to these professionals to establish connections and potentially secure a recommendation for future job opportunities.
